RF Microneedling

Morpheus 8 is an advanced skin treatment that incorporates Microneedling and fractionated Radio Frequency (RF) to achieve optimal skin rejuvenation. By stimulating the production of collagen, RF Microneedling rejuvenates the skin by remodeling tissue on a cellular level. RF Microneedling is a great way to improve your appearance without surgery or downtime and scores of men and women have used this high-tech facial to reduce fine lines and wrinkles, tighten skin, correct skin tone and texture, and improve scarring (such as acne scars and stretch marks.)

How does Microneedling with Radio Frequency work?

The Morpheus 8 combines two advanced technologies, Fractionated Radio Frequency and Microneedling into one facial or body treatment. During the procedure, microscopic needles puncture the skin to stimulate natural skin rejuvenation via a process known as controlled wound healing. In response, the body creates more collagen and elastin (the structural proteins that keep the skin young and healthy-looking.)
In addition to the micro-needles, Morpheus 8 uses Fractionated RF energy to heat the tissue. This causes the tissue to contract (tighten) and further increases the production of collagen.
Unlike other RF Microneedling treatments that superficially penetrate the skin, Morpheus 8 targets the deepest layers of skin tissue. The device is FDA cleared to rejuvenate the skin by subdermal adipose remodeling - remodeling of fat below the skin’s surface – to achieve optimal results. Remodeling fatty tissue is especially effective for shaping and contouring the lower face and neck. The treatment is commonly utilized to improve the appearance of jowls, wrinkling, skin laxity, and fat accumulation in the submental–neck and chin–region.

What skin conditions can Microneedling improve?

RF Micro-needling with the Morpheus 8 can rejuvenate skin and repair common blemishes on the face and body. Popular treatment applications include:

  • Improving fine lines and wrinkles;
  • Tightening skin (both lose facial skin & skin laxity on the body);
  • Diminishing the appearance of acne scars;
  • Fading stretch marks;
  • Correcting skin tone;
  • Smoothing skin texture;
  • Contouring the submental region.
